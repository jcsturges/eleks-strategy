# ELEKS New Brand Strategy

## What This Is

A working strategy project for designing and launching a **new separate brand** — owned by ELEKS but independently positioned — focused on **agentic AI and data/AI advisory services** for US enterprise clients.

The core premise: ELEKS has 34 years of offshore engineering credibility and deep AI/data technical capability, but the market perceives them as an execution partner, not a strategic advisor. A separate brand solves the buyer mismatch, pricing ceiling, and talent attraction problems that prevent ELEKS from competing for advisory work against Big 4 and MBB firms.

## Strategic Context

- **Parent Company:** ELEKS ($120M revenue, 2,100+ employees, founded 1991 in Ukraine)
- **Goal:** New brand generating $10M–$15M revenue by Year 3, contributing to ELEKS' target of $240M by 2031
- **Target Clients:** US-based upper mid-market and Fortune 450 companies ($500M–$50B revenue)
- **Focus Verticals:** Financial services, healthcare/life sciences, energy, government
- **Positioning:** Premium advisory firm (value pricing $150–$500/hr) vs. ELEKS' T&M model ($75–$150/hr)

## Deliverables Being Built

1. **Organizational Design & Hiring Plan** — Phased headcount plan (Year 1–3), roles, comp ranges, hiring timeline
2. **Compensation Proposal** — Founding leader package with base, bonus, equity/profit share, sign-on
3. **Supporting Analysis** — Market sizing, competitive landscape, financial model, risk mitigation
