## The Strategic Challenge

### Why a Separate Brand?
1. **Market Perception:** ELEKS = offshore engineering execution, not strategic advisory
2. **Buyer Mismatch:** CIOs/CTOs hire McKinsey/Deloitte for strategy, not IT services firms
3. **Pricing Ceiling:** Can't charge advisory rates ($250-500/hr) under ELEKS brand
4. **Talent Attraction:** Top US-based consultants won't join "Ukrainian outsourcer"

### Why Now?
1. **Agentic AI is greenfield:** No established "agentic AI advisory" category leader
2. **ELEKS has technical credibility:** AI R&D lab, MLOps/LLMOps capabilities, 100+ data scientists
3. **Market timing:** Enterprise buyers need help with *where* and *how* to deploy agents (business question, not tech)
4. **Competitive advantage:** ELEKS' engineering depth = credible implementation, not just slides
