## Risk Premium Calculations

### Factors That Increase Compensation Requirements

| Risk Factor | Compensation Premium |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Greenfield practice (no existing infrastructure) | +15-20% base OR +10% higher bonus target |
| No guaranteed budget | +20-30% base OR sign-on bonus |
| Geopolitical risk (Ukraine exposure) | +10-15% base |
| Career risk (failed practice = resume gap) | +15-20% OR sign-on bonus |
| Complex org (13 countries, offshore model) | +10% base |
| **Total risk premium** | **+70K - +100K over stable role** |

### De-Risking Factors That Reduce Premium

| De-Risking Factor | Premium Reduction |
|-------------------|-------------------|
| Approved multi-year budget | -10% premium |
| Board-level champion (CEO/CTO support) | -10% premium |
| Proven concept (pilot clients lined up) | -15% premium |
| Equity/profit share upside | -20% premium |

## Comparable Role: "ELEKS New Brand - Founding Leader"

### Market Positioning
This role is most comparable to:
- **Greenfield practice leader** at IT services firm (Cognizant, Wipro model)
- **Founding director** at boutique AI consultancy
- **VP of new ventures** at mid-tier consulting firm

### Appropriate Compensation Range

**Base Salary:** $210K - $250K
- Lower end if significant equity/profit share
- Higher end if minimal long-term incentive

**Target Bonus:** 25-40% of base ($52K - $100K)
- Tied to: Brand revenue, client acquisition, team build

**Upside Bonus:** Up to 50-60% of base if stretch goals hit

**Equity/Profit Share:**
- **Option A:** 5-10% of brand EBITDA (kicks in Year 2)
- **Option B:** 0.5-1% phantom equity in ELEKS (long-term, illiquid)
- **Option C:** None, but bonus ceiling at 60%+ of base

**Sign-On Bonus:** $25K - $50K (one-time, covers transition risk)

**Total Comp Scenarios:**

| Scenario | Base | Bonus | Equity/Profit | Total Year 1 | Total Year 3 |
|----------|------|-------|---------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Conservative** | $210K | $50K | None | $260K | $280K |
| **Target** | $230K | $70K | 5% EBITDA | $300K | $450K |
| **Aggressive** | $250K | $100K | 8% EBITDA | $350K | $600K |
