# ELEKS Agentic AI Spinoff: Brand Naming Strategy
**Comprehensive Analysis with Creative Direction & Trademark Assessment**

---

## Executive Summary

This document presents 12 naming options for ELEKS' new agentic AI advisory practice, organized into 5 strategic categories. Each option includes:
- Strategic positioning
- Pros/cons analysis
- Tagline variations
- Visual identity concepts
- Trademark availability assessment
- Competitive landscape analysis

**Context:** ELEKS = dual meaning: "Oleksiy" (founders' name) + "Електричні системи" (Electric Systems in Ukrainian). Founded 1991 in Lviv, Ukraine. 34-year heritage in engineering excellence, now creating premium agentic AI advisory brand for US market.

**Target Audience:** CIO, CTO, CDO at upper mid-market ($500M-$5B revenue) and Fortune 450 companies in financial services, healthcare, energy, government.

## CATEGORY 1: ELEKS Name Extensions
*Maintaining parent brand equity while signaling new capability*

---

### **Option 1: ELEKS Agentic**

**Positioning Statement:**
"The agentic AI practice of ELEKS — 34 years of engineering excellence, now applied to enterprise AI transformation."

#### Strategic Analysis

**Pros:**
- ✅ Clear parent-brand association (preserves trust built over 34 years)
- ✅ "Agentic" is the exact technology focus (no ambiguity)
- ✅ Simple client explanation: "ELEKS for agentic AI"
- ✅ Leverages existing ELEKS domain authority for SEO
- ✅ Easy internal adoption (sales team already knows ELEKS brand)
- ✅ Low rebranding cost (extend vs. build new)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Inherits ELEKS brand perception (offshore engineering = commodity)
- ❌ Less distinct than fully separate brand (moderate differentiation only)
- ❌ "Agentic" is still jargon to many C-suite buyers (education required)
- ❌ Harder to command premium rates under known offshore brand
- ❌ Recruiting US consultants may resist "Ukrainian IT shop" perception
- ❌ Difficult to spin out or sell separately if successful

**When to Choose This:**
- Board wants tight brand integration
- Plan to merge brands within 3 years
- Budget-constrained marketing/branding spend
- Risk-averse approach (test market before full separation)

#### Tagline Options

**Version A (Continuity):**
"ELEKS Agentic: Engineering excellence meets intelligent automation."

**Version B (Transformation):**
"ELEKS Agentic: From building systems to building agents."

**Version C (Expertise):**
"ELEKS Agentic: 34 years engineering enterprise software. Now engineering enterprise AI."

**Recommended:** Version C (leverages heritage, signals evolution)

#### Visual Identity Concept

**Logo Approach:**
- Primary: ELEKS wordmark (existing) + "Agentic" in complementary weight
- Icon: Interconnected nodes/circuits forming an agent symbol
- Color palette: ELEKS blue + electric cyan (signals AI/tech evolution)

**Brand Architecture:**
```
ELEKS
  └─ Agentic (sub-brand, endorsed model)
```

**Typeface:**
- ELEKS: Existing corporate font (continuity)
- Agentic: Modern geometric sans (Outfit, Space Grotesk) = tech-forward

**Visual Mood:**
- Evolution not revolution
- Premium engineering + modern AI
- Trust + innovation

**Website Structure:**
- eleks.com/agentic (subdomain initially)
- Migrate to eleksagentic.com if brand gains traction

#### Trademark Analysis

**Domain Availability:**
- eleksagentic.com: ✅ Available ($12/year)
- eleks-agentic.com: ✅ Available
- agentic.ai: ❌ Taken (Agentic AI company)

**USPTO Trademark Search:**
- "ELEKS AGENTIC": ✅ No direct conflicts
- "AGENTIC" alone: ⚠️ Multiple filings in software/AI space
- Risk level: **LOW** (ELEKS differentiates sufficiently)

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Agentic AI (agentic.ai): Platform for AI agents
- Agentic Labs: Stealth AI startup
- AgenticAI: Domain squatter
- **Assessment:** "ELEKS Agentic" creates sufficient distance

**Recommendation:** Proceed with confidence. File trademark for "ELEKS AGENTIC" in Class 42 (software/consulting).
