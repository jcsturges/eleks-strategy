## Strategic Risks & Challenges

### Market Risks
1. **Commoditization:** Offshore engineering increasingly competitive, margin pressure
2. **Client concentration:** Unknown but likely significant (common in IT services)
3. **Talent drain:** 65,000+ Ukrainian tech workers emigrated post-2022
4. **Geopolitical:** Ukraine war escalation, energy infrastructure attacks

### Operational Risks
1. **Sales/delivery misalignment:** Glassdoor reviews suggest sales selling staff aug, not higher-value work
2. **Compensation pressure:** Frozen raises creating retention risk
3. **Geographic complexity:** Managing 18 offices across 8 time zones
4. **Ukraine dependency:** Still 40-50% of workforce in Ukraine

### Strategic Transformation Risks
1. **Moving upstream is hard:** Most offshore firms fail to establish advisory credibility
2. **Internal resistance:** Sales team incentivized to sell what they know (engineering, not consulting)
3. **Talent mismatch:** Engineers ≠ consultants (different skillsets, career motivations)
4. **Brand equity:** 34 years as "ELEKS the engineering firm" hard to overcome

## Why a Separate Brand Makes Sense

### Brand Equity Problem
- **ELEKS = offshore engineering** in buyer minds (34 years of messaging)
- **Advisory buyers** (CIO, CTO) hire McKinsey, BCG, Deloitte for strategy
- **Rate ceiling:** Hard to charge $300-500/hr under offshore engineering brand
- **Talent attraction:** US-based senior consultants won't join "Ukrainian outsourcer"

### Separate Brand Advantages
1. **Clean slate:** No offshore/execution baggage
2. **Premium positioning:** Can charge advisory rates from day 1
3. **Talent magnet:** Easier to recruit top US consultants
4. **Clear swim lanes:** Advisory brand sells strategy, ELEKS implements
5. **Risk isolation:** If it fails, ELEKS brand unaffected

### Successful Analogues
- **Bain & Company → Bridgespan** (nonprofit advisory spinoff)
- **McKinsey → McKinsey Implementation** (delivery arm)
- **BCG → BCG Platinion** (technology implementation)
- **Slalom → Slalom Build** (separate engineering brand)
